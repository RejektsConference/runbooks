# Finance

Note when books close for the year for the overall series, and keep that deadline in mind.

## General Tasks

* Establish budget
* Maintain budget
* Perform periodic budget review
* Approve finance requests and manage expense reimbursements

## Timeline

### Pre-kickoff
* Establish budget
* Help with sponsorship prospectus creation

### Periodic/weekly
* Ensure all receipts are placed in the event's receipt directory
* Ensure all requests are evaluated
* Ensure all approved expenses are sent for payment or reimbursement

### 6 months out
* Review budget with all organizers to evaluate status

### 3 months out
* Review budget with all organizers to evaluate go/no-go

### Post conference
* Post budget evaluation
* Pass on budget sheets to next group
* Ensure last reimbursements are completed and books-close date is met on time
